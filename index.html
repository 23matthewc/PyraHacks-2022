Helping Children in Need Through Cancer Research

Donation Links

https://www.stjude.org/

St. Jude specializes in treatment of life-threatening diseases and childhood cancer.

https://www.acco.org/donate/#:~:text=MAKE%20A%20DONATION,toughest%20fights%20of%20their%20life.

Since 1970, ACCO has been helping families and children affected by cancer. Your donations can fund a specific program if you choose.

https://cookiesforkidscancer.org/

Cookies for Kid's Cancer funds cancer-battling research through cookie sales.

https://childrenscancer.org/donate/

Children's Cancer Research Fund takes monthly gifts in donation form. Their mission is to fund scientists with brilliant ideas around the world.

https://www.alexslemonade.org/about/different

Alex's Lemonade Stand is a foundation for cancer research. This foundation is the only NCI peer-reviewed funder designation childhood cancer research.

https://thenccs.org/

The NCCS supports families of those affected by cancer. They work with over 45,000 children and have distributed more than 68 million dollars over the program's lifetime.

https://www.stbaldricks.org/give

St. Baldrick's Foundation works on a one-time or monthly donation policy. They work with leading pediatric oncologists to decide what research to fund to help children with cancer, and they have funded the most grants out of any organization besides the US government.

hhttps://nationalpcf.org/

The NPCF is a non-profit devoted to fund research in order to make safer, more effective childhood cancer treatment.

https://www.childrenscancerfund.com/make-a-donation

Children's Cancer Fund is an organization hoping to improve treatment and care methods for North Texas children. Through fun events, they also aim to improve the lives of affected children and families.
